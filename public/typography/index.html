Typography

Typhoon typography guide

Overview

Typhoon uses two font types consistent with university brand standards, Merriweather and Nimbus Sans. Merriweather is used for all the headers within Typhoon and in specific situations. Nimbus Sans is used for the body font of Typhoon for its clean appeereance and easy reading. Visit university brand guidelines and Iowa States Web Theme for more information.
