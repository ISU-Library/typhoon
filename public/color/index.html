Color

Typhoon Color Palette

Overview

Typhoon uses a color pallete generated from three university markting approved colors. Due to the complexities of the web design these colors were expanded to create a variety of colors needed for good web design. The main colors are red and yellow with a blue for accent. Additionally there are a neural colors for general use and support colors for alerts and form confirmations.
